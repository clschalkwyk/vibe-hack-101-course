# Hardest Problem First

Every project has one piece most likely to break everything.

**Build it first.**

---

### 🃏 Pocket Poker — Commit-Reveal Mechanic
How do you prevent a player seeing the opponent's cards before reveal, while staying trustless on-chain? Solved first. Without it, there was no game.

### 🎨 MoodRingNFT — Wallet Read → AI Pipeline
Reading on-chain wallet history and piping it into an AI image generator. Solved end-to-end for one wallet before anything else was built.

---

**Proof-of-Concept Gate** — before building anything else:
- Core mechanic works in isolation
- Correct output observed at least once  
- Integration path is understood
