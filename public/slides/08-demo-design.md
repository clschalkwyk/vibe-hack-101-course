# Demo Design

**The demo is the product.** Not the code. Not the README.

---

## The 3-Minute Arc

| Segment | Time | Content |
|---------|------|---------|
| Hook | 0:20 | The problem, stated sharply |
| Frame | 0:20 | What your project does — one sentence |
| Live Demo | 1:40 | Walk through the happy path |
| Impact | 0:20 | Who uses this, why it matters |
| CTA | 0:10 | What's next |

---

**❌ Weak:** "Hi, we built KuduPay, a stablecoin payment app for South Africa."

**✅ Strong:** "10M+ South Africans send money home monthly — losing 5–10% to fees. KuduPay fixes that with ZAR stablecoins on Lisk."
