# Pitch Simulation

The last 10% that wins. Rehearse until you own the room.

---

## The Simulation Format

**R1 — Full Run-Through** `5 min`
Present the full demo and pitch. No stopping. Time it.

**R2 — Hard Questions** `5 min`
Judge asks the most challenging questions possible. Answer live.

**R3 — Debrief** `10 min`
What landed? What felt unclear? What tripped you up?

**R4 — Adjusted Run** `5 min`
Incorporate feedback. Run again.

---

> **One-sentence pitch:** *[Project] helps [user] do [action] using [key technology].*
> 
> Practise until it comes out naturally in any conversation.
