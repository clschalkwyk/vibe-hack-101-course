# Rapid Prototyping

**Build the demo, not the product.**

Your goal is a working demonstration of the idea — not production-ready software.

---

## Build in This Order

1. **Core Mechanic** — Hardest thing (done)
2. **Data Flow** — End-to-end through the app
3. **Happy Path** — Exactly what you'll demo
4. **Polish** — UI, errors, loading states
5. **Nice-to-Haves** — Only if time permits

---

> **The 80% Rule:** If time is running out, cut the last 20% and invest that time in demo prep and pitch rehearsal instead.
