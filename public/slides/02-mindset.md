# Hackathon Mindset

Hackathons are **not** coding competitions.

They are **storytelling competitions** where code is the evidence.

---

- 💡 **Ideas** — Judges reward clarity and vision, not complexity
- ⚡ **Speed** — A finished 80% beats an unfinished 100% every time
- 🎬 **Story** — If it needs explaining, it's not demo-ready

---

> "Win the room before you win the judging." — Wikus Schalkwyk
