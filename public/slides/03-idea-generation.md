# Idea Generation

Great hackathon ideas come from one of **three places**:

---

### 📈 Sponsor Tracks
Follow the money. Read every prize track. Build exactly what they're rewarding.

### 💡 Your Own Friction
What frustrated you in the last 30 days? Tech can probably fix it.

### 🔄 The Remix
Something that works in one domain, applied to a new context.

---

**Wikus's wins:** MoodRingNFT · Pocket Poker · KuduPay

Each came from reading the track carefully and adding a local or novel angle.
